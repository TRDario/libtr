tr::Cursor::Cursor ( std::span< const std::byte > color,
std::span< const std::byte > mask,
glm::ivec2 size,
glm::ivec2 focus )

Creates a simple black-and-white cursor from color and mask bitfields.

Exceptions
CursorBadAllocIf allocating the cursor fails.
Parameters
[in]colorA span of bits where 0 is white and 1 black.
[in]maskA span of bits determining the mask: 1 is opaque, 0 + white = transparent, 0 + black = inverted color.
[in]sizeThe size of the cursor graphic, both coordinates must be multiples of 8 and match the sizes of the bitfields.
[in]focusThe focus point of the cursor (where the actual mouse position is relative to the graphic).
