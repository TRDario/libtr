template<StandardLayout T, std::size_t S>
auto tr::asMutObjects ( std::span< std::byte, S > bytes)
noexcept

Reinterprets a span of mutable bytes as a span of objects.

The span must be an integer multiple of the size of the object being cast into. If the span has a static length, the function will fail to compile, and if the span has a dynamic length, a failed assertion may be triggered.
